The main motivation behind this project is that to spread awareness regarding the forest fires occurring in the different regions of our country. We want to help the forest department of our country by developing this system so that they can spread the awareness of forest fires to the people staying near those areas.

This project helps to predict whether there will be a forest fire or not in a particular forest region. We will save a lot of time by just get updates about the next forest fire, and if there is one we will provide immediate preventions.
